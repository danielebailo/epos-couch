Help

A short help which explains briefly how to fill the database

LOGIN

  • click on the login button (upper right)
  • insert your user and pass
  • click on "edit RI" --> you'll be redirected to the edit panel

INCLUDE NEW RESEARCH INFRASTRUCTURE

  • click "Add RI" on the top right corner of the RI list

ADD/UPDATE RESEARCH INFRASTRUCTURE

To add details to your research infrastructure:
  • login and go to "edit RI"
  • find your RI or add a new one (add RI button on the top right corner above the list of RIs)
  • edit it (modifying icon on the left)
  • add facilities with the 'ADD ITEM' button.
