WG2 - Volcano Observations

Volcano Observatories present an heterogeneity of situations, and include several different kind of network, instruments, facilities. To insert the data please use the following available forms:

  • Seismic Network: Here you can store the seismic network information. Please make sure that the network you are declaring is not already declared in other RIs.
