  1. First time here?

    Click 'next' and follow the tutorial...
    or close this box

  2. R.I.D.E. Research Infrastructure Database for EPOS

    ...let's start: this page presents a simple two column structure...

  3. Left Column

    On this column you have the Research Infrastructures (RIs) list

  4. Rigth column

    Here you have a statistic tool, which enables you to carry on statistics ONLY on the RIs listed on the left

  5. RI PopUp

    Just click on any RI listed here below (try now!), and a pop-up containing RIs info will show up.

    Tip: log in if you have an account, and you'll have access to additional infos

  6. Filter bar

    This search menu enables you to filter the RIs listed just below. Write, just now, 'country:italy', hit enter...
    ..Only italian RIs are now listed.

    Tip: All field names in the RI pop-ups can be used as filters!

  7. First Statistic

    1. Select 'general info:ri name' on y-axis
    2. Select 'WG' on x-axis
